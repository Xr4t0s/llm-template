# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### 1. What is LLM Studio?
LLM Studio is an integrated platform designed to simplify Web3 project management through automation, security, and analytics.

### 2. Who can benefit from LLM Studio?
Web3 developers, project managers, startups, and enterprises building decentralized applications.

### 3. Does LLM Studio have a native token?
No, the platform operates under a subscription-based utility model without token issuance.

### 4. How do I start using LLM Studio?
Sign up on the platform, create your project workspace, and follow the onboarding guide to configure your workflows.

### 5. What blockchain networks are supported?
Currently, Ethereum mainnet is supported; multi-chain integrations are planned.

### 6. Is my project data secure?
Yes, we apply best-in-class encryption and security measures to protect your data.

### 7. Can I customize automation workflows?
Yes, users can customize task automation to fit specific project needs.

### 8. What kind of analytics does LLM Studio provide?
Real-time dashboards with metrics on project activity, transaction statuses, and user engagement.

### 9. How often is the platform updated?
We release updates regularly based on user feedback and technological advances.

### 10. Is there customer support?
Yes, we provide dedicated support for all subscription tiers.

### 11. Can I integrate LLM Studio with other tools?
Yes, it supports integrations with common Web3 development and deployment platforms.

### 12. What are the pricing plans?
Plans vary based on features and scale; details are available on our pricing page.

### 13. How can I provide feedback or suggestions?
Users are encouraged to engage with our community channels or contact support directly.

### 14. Will LLM Studio support governance?
Governance modules are planned for future releases.

### 15. How do you ensure transparency?
Open documentation, clear changelogs, and community involvement ensure transparency.
