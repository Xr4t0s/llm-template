# Roadmap for Unnamed

## Project Phases and Timeline

### Phase 1: Foundation (Months 1-3)
- Concept development focused on glitch memes and cultural experimentation.
- Branding including creation and introduction of the mascot Glitch.
- Community engagement and onboarding.

### Phase 2: Community Expansion (Months 4-6)
- Launch of decentralized collaboration platform.
- Initiate community-driven meme and glitch art experiments.
- Forge partnerships with Web3 creators and innovators.

### Phase 3: Growth and Innovation (Months 7-12)
- Deployment of new creative tools and experimental formats.
- Expansion of community membership and active participants.
- Development of governance mechanisms and incentive models.

### Phase 4: Sustainable Ecosystem (Year 2+)
- Growth of global creator collaborations.
- Integration with wider Web3 ecosystems.
- Continuous enhancement of participation rewards and creative tools.

## Key Milestones
- Brand and mascot launch.
- Platform beta rollout.
- Completion of initial major cultural experiment.
- Community reaches 1,000+ active creators.

## Goals
- **Short-term:** Build a foundation and core community.
- **Long-term:** Foster a sustainable, influential cultural ecosystem.

## Future Expansion Plans
- Broaden creative scope with advanced experiments.
- Foster interoperability and adoption in broader Web3.
- Facilitate global participation and cultural bridging.

---

Progress through every phase marks deeper engagement and wider cultural impact on digital expression.
